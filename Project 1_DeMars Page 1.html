Strength

Across the 8 tropical basins, there are 4 systems that are used to grade tropical cyclone strength. The Atlantic, West Pacific, and Central Pacific basins use the Saffir Simpon Wind Scale, which only takes into account wind speed instead of other dangerous hazards.

Saffir Simpson Wind Scale

Category 1
74-95mph winds
Category 2
96-110mph winds
Category 3
111-129mph winds
Category 4
130-156mph winds
Category 5
157mph winds or higher
